About US

We try to reduce students stress somewhat by heping them if they lost something precious on the college/hostle grounds. There are two sections where you can go to one is the LOST section and the other is the FOUND section. You can inform other people about what precious belonging you’ve lost and ask your fellow students for help and if you’ve found something then you can be of help to somebody else. Both the sections have their respective instructions. And the faculty involvement in this procedure is about nill, so it also helps in creating a strong bond between the kids and also to maintain transparency.
OUR MISSION..
Our mission is to make the students community more trustworthy and faithful and this place a safe space for all of your precious belongings. We all know the students have to bring many sorts of precious belongings to college for many reasons and there are many chances of loosing it which might give the students some permanent scars because who knows what insane amount of value a little thing might hold in a person’s life but don’t worry that’s where our page and your fellow collegemates come forward to help you ! We don’t want you to be stressed if you’ve lost something on the college and hostel grounds we just want you to fill the form under the LOST section so that we can get you out of this stress ASAP. Even this page is created and managed by some of the students, so your fellow collegemates have already started helping you in a way.
